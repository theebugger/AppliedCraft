Philosophy

The Craft

Practitioner Depth

Built from experience making product decisions, not from observing people who make them. Every framework here has been pressure-tested against actual constraints, actual stakeholders, actual deadlines.

Opinionated Clarity

Positions are more useful than summaries. If you can't disagree with an essay, the essay didn't say anything. Neutral coverage of everything is a service to no one.

Decision-Ready

Every piece ends with something you can actually do. The gap between "interesting" and "useful" is the gap between reading and deciding. This closes it.

About

Applied Craft is written by a product leader who has spent years at the intersection of product strategy and artificial intelligence.

This is not journalism. It's not consulting. It's one practitioner's attempt to think clearly about hard problems in public, and to share those frameworks with others facing the same decisions.

The name says what it is: the craft of applying ideas to real work. Starting with AI. Eventually, more.
